Final Project Summary

Purpose

My project is a single-page personal website. The first section is a home screen with my name as a title. There is also a navigation bar that will take the user to the other sections in the site. The next section is an about me section with a picture of me and a brief description of me and my interests. The third section is a Projects/Experience section. This section has an iframe, where the user has the option to view my resume or cover letter. There are also links to my LinkedIn and GitHub profiles. The fourth section is the section dedicated to my blogs. This page has links to 6 of my written blog posts, and the blog posts will open in separate tabs. The final section is a contact form for if the user wants to reach out to me personally, as well as links to some of my social media accounts.

Extras

- iframe in Projects section

- Contact form in last section that submits to confirmation page

- Scroll Animations between each section: JS Plugin
